James Eugene Carrey Born on January 17, 1962 Canadian-American Actor, Comedian,
Writer, Producer, and Artist

Born in the Canadian town of Newmarket, Ontario, on January 17, 1962. The youngest of four children, he spent his teenage years working as a janitor. He was a performer from day one and started performing in front of his high school peers. As he got older, he took his comedic skills to Los Angeles where he was soon discovered at the Comedy Store and his life would change for the better.
At the age of 19, he began acting in films and televisions. He scored an immediate hit with Ace Ventura: Pet Detective and had continued box-office success with Dumb and Dumber and The Mask (all 1994). His performance earned Carrey the first of several Golden Globe Award nominations.
