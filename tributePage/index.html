Jim Carrey

“It’s Better to Fail at What You Love Than Fail at What You Don’t.”


James Eugene Carrey
Canadian-American Actor, Comedian,
Writer, Producer, and Artist

1962 Born on January 17 in Ontario, Canada

1978 Started performing in front of his high school peers

1981 Took his comedic skills to Los Angeles

1983 Discovered at the Comedy Store in Los Angeles

1984 Began acting in films and televisions

1986 Marries Melissa Womer

1994 Starred in Ace Ventura: Pet Detective

1994 First Golden Globe for Best Actor

1997 Second Golden Globe

2003 Starred in hit movie Bruce Almighty

2008 Was in movie Yes Man

2017 Delivered commencement speech at Maharishi University of Management
